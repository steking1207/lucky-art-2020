Q&A
Q
How to purchase a ticket?
You can go to our website to purchase a ticket online, or you can purchase a ticket at our ticket counter in Lucky Art Crayonology.
Q
Is it possible not to participate in the Do-it-yourself (DIY) activities but to accompany others?
Either adults or children are all required to purchase a ticket to enter the Do-it-yourself (DIY) activity areas. Tickets include four Do-it-yourself (DIY) activities, and each of the activities cannot be purchase separately. In order to ensure the safety of children in the Lucky Art Crayonology, children must be accompanied by an adult when entering the venue, and they cannot purchase the tickets separately.
Q
Can I visit the Lucky Art Crayonology for free?
You are welcome to visit the Lobby, Lucky Art Introduction, and Gift Shop areas for free. Tickets are required at the Do-it-yourself (DIY) activity areas.
Q
How old is it suitable for admission?
Lucky Art Crayonology welcomes friends and children of all ages. Children over 3 years old will be more familiar with the Do-it-yourself (DIY) contents. (Children under the age of 3 are eligible to have free admission. The Do-it-yourself (DIY) activities excluded)
Q
How long do the Do-it-yourself (DIY) activities take?
A general full fare ticket includes 4 Do-it-yourself (DIY) activities. Each activity takes approximately half an hour, and it takes approximately two hours in total. The opening hours of the Lucky Art Crayonology are 10:00 am to 5:00 pm Monday to Thursday and 10:00 am to 7:00 pm Friday to Sunday. We might adjust the business hour according to actual business conditions. Please follow the admission time announced by the on-site staff after ticket purchase.
Q
Can I bring food and drink inside?
You cannot bring food to the Do-it-yourself (DIY) activity areas. The food will worsen the cleanliness of the areas and negatively affects other visitors during their activities. Outside food and alcoholic beverages are forbidden in the entire dining area.
Q
Are pets allowed to enter the venue?
We will provide your pet a space where you can keep it safely during your visit. Small and medium pets need to be kept in the pet bags or carts. (Except guide dogs)
Q
Can I leave Lucky Art Crayonology and then come back later?
Yes, you can re-enter the venue with the ticket that purchased the same day. Please remember that you can only experience once in each Do-in-yourself (DIY) activity. Please notify the counter staff for re-entering.
Q
Do I need to purchase a parking space? Is it possible to reserve a parking space?
Lucky Art Crayonology contains 57 free parking spaces which include 4 accessible parking spaces. (The parking lot is for Lucky Art Crayonology visitors ONLY. Non-visitors are not allowed to park).
Q
Does the Lucky Art Crayonology provide storage space?
The Lucky Art Crayonology provides free lockers for the visitors to use. Please ask the counter staff for the service. Please take responsibility for your personal belongings by yourself. DO NOT leave your valuables unattended in the venue. If there is any stolen or loss, we will NOT be responsible for that situation.
